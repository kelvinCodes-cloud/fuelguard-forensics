Tracker vs Fuel Sensor They are different devices working together.

Tracker: the Teltonika GPS/GSM unit. It sends location, speed, time, and online/offline signal.

Fuel sensor: the tank measuring device. It sends fuel level changes in litres.

Forensics: the alert becomes suspicious when tracker signal disappears and fuel level drops before the truck comes back online.

Hardware note Teltonika can handle this setup.

Use a Teltonika tracker for GPS/GSM data, then connect a supported fuel sensor through the device input/RS232/RS485 depending on the model.

This demo keeps both fields separate so the project explanation is clear during defence.

Status meaning Active · Online · Offline

Active: truck is registered and allowed in the system.

Online: tracker is currently reporting data.

Offline: tracker has stopped reporting or signal is lost.
