How to explain tracker and fuel sensor

Tracker: The Teltonika unit is the communication and location device. It tells the dashboard where the truck is, whether it is moving, and whether it is online or offline.

Fuel sensor: The fuel sensor is mounted on/in the tank and measures fuel level. It tells the system how many litres are available and whether a sudden drop happened.

Together: If the tracker goes offline and the fuel sensor shows a large drop before the truck returns online, the system flags a possible fuel theft event for forensic review.
